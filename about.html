Who We Are

Connecting You to
Faith, Community, & Hope

Christ Temple International Ministry was founded with a clear, heaven-born mandate: to make Christ known, to establish believers in their faith, and to build a vibrant, loving community that reflects the light of God. Over the years, we have grown into a diverse family representing people from all walks of life, united by our devotion to Jesus.

"We are not just a church; we are a family where you can belong, grow, and serve the purpose of God for your generation."

Whether you are seeking answers, looking for a spiritual home, or desiring to deepen your walk with God, Christ Temple is a place where you will receive sound biblical teaching, experience genuine fellowship, and find opportunities to make a difference in our world.

Our Mission

To preach the uncompromised gospel of Jesus Christ, build a community of committed disciples, and reach the world with the transforming message of love, healing, and salvation.

Our Vision

To see lives transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit, establishing strong families, vibrant local assemblies, and a lasting legacy of faith that influences generations to come.

OUR CORE VALUES

01

JESUS IS OUR
MESSAGE

Jesus is unrivaled in history and eternity. He is God and He is good. His Name is the only name that saves. His power is the only power that can raise us from the dead.

02

LOVE IS OUR
COMMAND

We believe that following Jesus leads us to a place of uncommon unity where we lay aside the labels that usually divide us for something much more life giving.

03

PEOPLE ARE OUR
PASSION

We believe that every person is created in the image of God and is deeply loved. We are passionate about loving our neighbors, serving our city, and reaching the world.
